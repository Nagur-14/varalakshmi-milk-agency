About VaraLakshmi Milk Agency

🐄

VaraLakshmi Milk Agency has been serving the community of Chilakaluripet with the finest quality dairy products for over a decade. We work directly with local farmers in Palnadu District to bring you milk that is fresh, pure, and natural — collected every morning and delivered to your door.

Our range includes fresh milk varieties, butter, cream, ghee, ice cream, and daily essentials — all sourced with care and delivered with a smile.
